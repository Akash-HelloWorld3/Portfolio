Personal Electrical Women Safety Device
  • Working at nights has been a trend lately in India for both men and women. As a women at some point of career life they have been exposed to sexism, workplace bullying or even harassment by male superiors. Newspaper headlines have become common these days where women become victims of recriminations from their alleged sexual abusers, stating that the woman who claims to be harassed or inappropriately touched is lying to tarnish the image of the abuser. It has been found that women working during nights are no way safe; neither on roads, nor in public transports that are provided to them at their respective workplaces. At these times there isn’t a proper way for a women to communicate with the higher authorities/police about incident/location at that instance. Even if it is happening in an area with no network connectivity there is no efficient way to send her location/status so that the Police can track her down to rescue her. Due to this there is a lot of cases rising in sexual harassement/ Assault/ rape/ kidnapping. Even after finding the culprit the police does not possess enough information for evidence submission.
  • At Incidents of sexual harassment/Assault the person needs to say the keyword “ Amber Help ” (AMBER App is integrated with AI Voice recognition) inorder to activate the AMBER App which accesses the GPS Location and also records the Voice. This Live GPS location is shared through SMS of her Emergency contacts and the Data containing her GPS location and Voice record information is stored in the cloud which can be accessed by the higher authorities to track her down. The Unique feature of AMBER App is, it transfers the GPS location to the nearest police station using LoRa Module, which works even when there is no network connectivity. This LoRa module is connected to the smartphone via Bluetooth with (2000 MAH) LI-ION battery as a power source. The message sent through the LoRa has a range of 5-7 Km, which means every 5 Km surrounding there will be a police station which has a receiver end to get the final message. This system range can be expanded when there are several other devices nearby the sender device. When the police receives the final message they can track and rescue her right before the incident takes place. This AMBER App prevents sexual harassment/Assault/Abduction leading to rape and can even track the person when they are being kidnapped. The police can access the voice record from the portal for further investigation and submission of evidence.
